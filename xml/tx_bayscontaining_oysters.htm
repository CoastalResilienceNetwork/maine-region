Description:
Abstract:
Minor bays used by the coastal fisheries division in data collection activities.
Purpose:
To provide better resolution for mapping harvest monitoring database.
Supplemental_Information:
Procedures_Used Selected arcs out of primary source coverage (esi-beg(nwi)) that composed minor bays in CF-TPWD operations manual. If a minorbay was not present in the primary coverage, then secondary coverages were referred to in this order: TxDOT, NOAA, USGS, DOQQ. Each minorbay's boundary was selected from it's parent coverage, then was edited and built to form a polygon. Then appended to and attributed.
Revisions This is revision number 2. The 1st was flawed because many contiguous polygons were not represented as such.
Reviews_Applied_to_Data Two interns performed the initial revisions. These were verified or corrected.
