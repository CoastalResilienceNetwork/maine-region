    Abstract:
      This theme contains distribution of oyster bars in
      Apalachicola Bay, East Bay, St. George Sound, and St.
      Vincent Sound.
    Purpose:
      The purpose is to assist planning, management, and research
      in the area by showing distribution of this habitat
      component and economically important resource.

      this coverage. Notes from the NWFWMD refer to "FDEP and
      Army Corps, 1984". The people at the WMD originally
      responsible for the coverage are no longer there. We feel
      that this coverage is in need of update with modern methods.
