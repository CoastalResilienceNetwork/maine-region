Description:
Abstract:
Wetland areas mapped by the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service's National Wetlands Inventory based on 1992-93 photography within the areas of certain U.S. Geological Survey 1:24,000 quads in coastal counties of Texas. Digital line graph files containing these quad areas were converted to ARC/INFO and appended into a single coverage by Texas General Land Office.
Purpose:
Cartographic display and spatial analysis of wetland and open water areas. Data may not accurately depict current conditions.
Supplemental_Information:
Polygons of wetland areas and types were delineated by U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service specialists based on interpretation of aerial photography of various scales (generally 1:40,000 or larger). Polygons may not have been field checked.
